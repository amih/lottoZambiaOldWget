What are the best places to visit in Africa?

Assuming that you have settled on Africa as your destination for a dream holiday, you have unlimited destinations. Here are the top 5 that a lotto millionaire from Zambia should visit:

Zanzibar is one of the single most attractive destinations in Africa. It is an island on the east coast of Africa. It is a unique island that only has the richest people from all over the world visiting.

A visit to Zanzibar is one that has a guarantee of fun and exciting adventures. There are quiet beaches like Pongwe and Matemwe where you will have the best beach life in Africa. You are probably living in Africa but haven’t experienced nature at its best. A visit to the Serengeti will change your view of safari in Africa. This is an amazing city in South Africa where you will experience Africa at its best. This is a group of 115 islands on the Indian Ocean that attracts tourists from all over the world. It is a true getaway for people who are looking for ideal islands to relax after winning huge cash prizes playing the lottery.
