3 the most popular lotto strategies

Lottery strategies have helped several lottery players to play the lottery game. It has also helped them to improve the chances of winning a jackpot especially those who are just new to the game. So, what is a lottery strategy? A lottery strategy is a self-developed system used for choosing the best lottery numbers to play and to pick the one that has the highest chance of hitting the jackpot.


If you are interested in winning BIG, check out the three most popular lotto strategies in the lottery world.


1. IDENTIFYING HOT, COLD, AND OVERDUE NUMBERS

Before you can use this strategy, you need to first analyze the results of the lottery game that you want to play and identify the kind of numbers they are from the results. While analyzing these results, check the numbers that have recent draws and the ones that do not. From the analyzed draws, select at least 50 numbers from it.

 

The hot numbers are the numbers you selected most frequently during the drawings you’ve just analyzed. You can take these numbers as your “lucky” numbers. The cold numbers are the numbers you least frequently selected from the total drawings. The cold numbers may also have been recently drawn from the analyzed draws but since they could be below the average drawn numbers, they are considered to be “unlucky” numbers. The Overdue numbers are the numbers that have not been seen in recent weeks.

Most players have claimed that generating a set of winning numbers by combining hot, cold, and overdue numbers can hit a jackpot in no time. Don’t live in doubt, give this strategy a trial in your next play. Click here to read more about the hot and cold numbers strategy.


2. THE WHEELING SYSTEM

The wheeling system functions by aiming at a sure number of common numbers and then mixing these numbers with any other extra possible number to create many tickets which will have higher chances of hitting lower prizes of a lottery game.

For instance, when you want to play a lottery game where you have to get six correctly out of 69 possible numbers before you can win (this game is sometimes referred to as the 6/69 lottery), firstly you can start by identifying the four "hot" numbers.

Then purchase the tickets that include these four numbers with any extra 45 possible numbers.

If you identified 4, 13, 39, and 41 as the “hot” numbers (lucky numbers), then your lottery wheeling strategy would be to purchase tickets that look like:

1, 2, 4, 13, 39, 41
1, 3, 4, 13, 39, 41
1, 5, 13, 39, 41
1, 6, 4, 13, 39, 41
1, 7, 4, 13, 39, 41

and so forth. This implies that players must buy hundreds of tickets in total before applying this strategy.
The cost of playing the wheeling strategy can be reduced in a few different ways: Pick five numbers from draws and wheel the sixth.

Instead of play a pick-six game like Mega Millions or Powerball, play a pick-three or pick-four lottery game.
Use a lottery organization that purchases the wheels and sells “shares” to her members for any winning percent.
Try to form a pool of lottery games.

3. USING MATHEMATICAL SEQUENCES TO CRACK THE LOTTERY

Some lottery players analyze the series of lottery games to get the winning lottery numbers from it. This is a mathematical-based strategy that involves decoding a sequence of lottery games They do this by finding a repeated kind of pattern in the previous and recent draws. For instance, the Delta Lottery System deals with the average distance between winning lottery numbers.

This is just an illustration but there are more than hundreds of ways you can find a pattern that will give you the right prediction and luck. This can be achieved by constant observation of the recent winning numbers displayed on any lottery website. By during so regularly, you might find a nice pattern that would give you your victory.
