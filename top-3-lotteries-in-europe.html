TOP 3 Lotteries in Europe

The lottery is one of the oldest forms of entertainment in the world. People love to play the lottery everywhere in the world. Europe is one of the earliest places where lotteries became extremely popular. Some of the world’s popular lotteries are created in Europe.

It is very exciting to play one of the transnational lotteries in Europe. However, the fun is only limited to players who choose top lotteries in Europe. Here are the top three lotteries in Europe that you can trust to play with.

 

SuperEnalotto

This is one of the oldest lottery games in Europe. The lottery was started in 1997 in Italy and has since evolved to become one of Europe’s top lottery games. The popularity of this lottery game is mainly because of the huge winning prospects and the ease of playing the game. Here are some quick questions and answers that can guide you on how to play SuperEnalotto:

How much does a ticket cost?

A ticket costs €2 per line. Therefore, with this little amount, you can become the next millionaire in Europe.

How much can you win?

While playing SuperEnalotto, you can win over €100 million. However, this jackpot keeps on increasing weekly. The biggest amount ever to be won was €209 million.

How to play?

After you buy a ticket, you will choose six numbers from a pool of 90. The objective is to match the six numbers correctly. If you do so, you will win the jackpot prize. Otherwise, you can win other cash prizes for matching some numbers correctly.

Eurojackpot

Eurojackpot is a popular transnational lottery game in Europe. The lottery was launched in 2012 and has attracted many participating European countries. Here are some pertinent questions that will help you understand this popular lottery in Europe:

How much does a ticket cost?

Each ticket costs €2.

How much can you win?

With a ticket of €2, you can win as much as €90 million.

How to play Eurojackpot?

Playing the game is pretty simple. You pick five numbers from a pool of 50 numbers then add two extra numbers from a pool of 1-10. You win the jackpot prize if you match all the numbers correctly.

EuroMillions

EuroMillions is also a popular transnational lottery in Europe. It is a popular lottery because of the probable big winnings that players get. Here are some quick questions and answers about EuroMillions:

Cost of ticket: €2.5 per ticket.

How much can you win?

You can win a minimum of €17 million if you are playing for the jackpot. However, with the rollover, the jackpot prize can go up to €220 million.

How to play EuroMillions?

In this game, you choose a total of 7 numbers. You pick 5 numbers from a pool of 1-50 and 2 Lucky Star numbers from a pool of 1-12.

 

Can you play these games outside Europe?

All the top lotteries in Europe have an online option where players can purchase a ticket online. This allows players from countries outside Europe to participate in these exciting lotteries.
